When would you want to use a remote repository rather than keeping all your work
local?

    Because meaybe you are working on a different computer. Or there are more than one person working
    on the project. So in order to keep the work tidy, it's better to work on a remote repository.

Why might you want to always pull changes manually rather than having Git
automatically stay up-to-date with your remote repository?

    Because then you can work offline, or be able to sync your work with other parts of your team
    before pushing your commits to the cloud.
